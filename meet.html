Meet Mary Grace

Hi my name is Mary Grace Ellis. I am a graphic designer. I was born in New Orleans, Louisiana. I have always been in love with art since the day I could hold a crayon. Although my major is graphic design, I also enjoy painting, drawing, printing; pretty much anything to stretch my artistic talents. I currently am majoring in a Bachelor of Fine Arts: Graphic Design. I believe art is a gift and visual language that allows us to both communicate and express ourselves beyond words. I am passionate about my work and love to test my limits.
I am an aspiring graphic designer who is passionate about excelling in my work. I have completed a range of projects that test my limits and got me into the place I am today. I am passionate about pursuing a career in graphic design and want to take any chance given to me.
